# Ecommerce-Sales-Analysis
Ecommerce-Sales-Analysis Dashbord


## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard developed entirely in Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, order trends, product category performance, and regional sales distribution.

The objective of this dashboard is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights using Excel's advanced analytics and visualization capabilities.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

Analyze overall sales, profit, orders, and quantity sold.

Monitor Year-over-Year (YoY) growth across key business metrics.

Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories.

Evaluate regional and state-wise sales performance.

Provide interactive filtering for deeper business analysis.

Support data-driven decision-making through visual insights.


## 📈 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a centralized view of business performance through the following components:

**KPI Cards**

Total Sales

Total Profit

Total Quantity Sold

Total Orders

Profit Margin %

Year-over-Year Growth Indicators

**Sales & Profit Trend Analysis**

Monthly sales and profit comparison

Trend analysis across the year

**Category-wise Profit Analysis**

Profit contribution by product category

Identification of high-performing categories

**Category-wise Sales Share**

Percentage contribution of each category to total sales

Pie chart visualization

**State-wise Sales Analysis**

Geographic sales distribution across the United States

State-level sales performance mapping

**Top 10 Sub-Categories**

Best-performing sub-categories based on sales

Comparative ranking visualization

### Interactive Filters


**Users can dynamically filter the dashboard by:**


Year

Region

Customer Segment
